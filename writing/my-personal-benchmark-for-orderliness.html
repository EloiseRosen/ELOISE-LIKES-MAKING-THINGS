ELOISE LIKES WRITING: MY PERSONAL BENCHMARK FOR ORDERLINESS

I have a personal benchmark for orderliness I apply to visitors to my apartment: some people put their shoes neatly in line with the other shoes by the door; some people put them neatly by the door, but not with the other shoes; some people just leave them wherever they took them off. It's maybe 33/33/33.

(Any of them is fine, I just notice it as a data point on someone's personality.)
