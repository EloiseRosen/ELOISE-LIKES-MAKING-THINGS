ELOISE LIKES WRITING: HELLOISE AND SWELLOISE

I can imagine the version of me that's 3 times worse than me (Helloise), and the version of me that's 3 times better (Swelloise). Helloise is withdrawn, rigid, cowardly, and particular. Swelloise is frighteningly competent, fun, and even more odd than me. I've found that "What Would Swelloise Do?" is a useful question to ask.
