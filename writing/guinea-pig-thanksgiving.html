ELOISE LIKES WRITING: GUINEA PIG THANKSGIVING

I didn't visit my parents for Thanksgiving this year, but I did make a Thanksgiving feast for the guinea pigs.
The menu: Cornlet “corn on the cob”, diced green bean and sweet potato casserole, salad with tomatoes, and pumpkin "pie" with cranberry slices

Special accommodations were made for Vesper to attend the meal from within her hiding container because she wasn't feeling up to leaving.

I expected that the guinea pigs may have poor table manners and had prepared for this possibility by glueing down the plates and the table cloth. However, I was not prepared for Aurora climbing on the table, stepping in the pumpkin pie, and stealing salad off of someone else's plate. No one fought about politics though, so I think it was still a win.
