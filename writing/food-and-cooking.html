ELOISE LIKES WRITING: FOOD AND COOKING

Some thoughts on food and cooking:

  1. I would have never guessed that cream would turn into whipped cream and then butter just from mixing it a lot. It makes me wonder if there are other things that would turn into something good that we just haven't tried mixing yet.
  2. I've long been suspicious that recipes tend to have extra steps that aren't really necessary (that is, you wouldn't be able to tell the difference in a blind taste test). I think when you're writing a recipe you're not really in an efficient and critical mindset, you're in a mindset of doing fun fancy things in the kitchen. The recipes I make a lot tend to get streamlined over time as I try shortcuts and find some parts that don't seem to matter.
  3. Between the fridge and freezer, I want a third compartment that's a little warmer than the freezer, so that ice cream isn't rock hard when you take it out.
  4. I haven't been able to find a frozen pizza brand that is half cheese half pepperoni (or more generally half vegetarian half meat), which surprises me. I would have thought there would be a market for that for couples or friends where one is vegetarian and the other isn't, and it would be cute.
