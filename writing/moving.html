ELOISE LIKES WRITING: MOVING

Every time I move, I go "wow this is way more work than I remember last time being. Surely I will remember this and now have accurate beliefs about the future." It's weird how strong the amnesia is given I've moved 9 times in my adult life, and how firmly the illusion persists that this time I'll remember for real. I've heard similar things about having children, that people forget just how bad the sleep deprivation really was until they're experiencing it again.
