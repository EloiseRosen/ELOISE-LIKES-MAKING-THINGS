ELOISE LIKES WRITING: SPORTS I LIKE

Some sports I like:

  • The modern pentathlon is an Olympic sport made up of five different events: fencing, 200m freestyle swimming, equestrian show jumping, and a final combined event of pistol shooting and cross country running. Besides the silliness of it, I like that it more holistically measures someone's athletic ability.
  • Obstacle swimming. From my youtube research it looks like they use a standardized course that's actually very boring, but the sport has fantastic potential. There should be weaving through traffic cones, and a water slide, and a tube, and a penny you have to fetch from the bottom of the pool.
