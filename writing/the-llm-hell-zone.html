ELOISE LIKES WRITING: THE LLM HELL ZONE

Recently I read some writing that was so bad I knew it wasn't written by an LLM. Interesting that writing being very bad, or very good, are both evidence of having been human-written: LLMs don't use awkward or stilted phrasings, don't have typos or grammatical mistakes, and don't use odd word choices. But LLMs also aren't good enough to produce great writing (yet). Kinda bad writing is the hell zone.
