ELOISE LIKES WRITING: SUNS

I wish we called stars "suns". When looking at the night sky I find it easy to mentally gloss over that every single star I see is an enormous fiery inferno, just like our home Sun, potentially with many planets circling it. Calling stars "suns" makes the vastness of the sky more salient.
