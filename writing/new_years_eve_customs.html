ELOISE LIKES WRITING: NEW YEAR'S EVE CUSTOMS

I've always thought it's weird that the way people traditionally celebrate New Year's, a holiday about fresh starts, practically ensures participants start the new year hung over, underslept, and too full of Cheetos. There's been a mix up somewhere - these customs are for a holiday called "Fuck Tomorrow". New Year's Eve is when we cook a nourishing dinner, have a nice reflection on the year, go to bed on time for a wonderful night of sleep, and wake up feeling rested and ready for a great start to the new year.

(To be clear, Fuck Tomorrow is also a great holiday, it just shouldn't be on December 31st.)
