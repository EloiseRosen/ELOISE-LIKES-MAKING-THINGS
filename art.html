ELOISE LIKES COSTUMES & SEWINGtop

For a hero-themed party I was the tug boat that freed the Ever Given from the canal. I painted a container ship toy to look like the Ever Given, put it on wheels, and dragged it behind me on a little leash all night. I made my tug boat costume out of cardboard and felt.

For a secrets-and-keys themed party, I carried a locked box, and had 51 keys attached around the bottom of my dress, only one of which opens the box. Party goers could pick a key to try, and if it opened the box they could have one of the secret prizes inside.

I was the photic sneeze reflex for a costume party. The dress is made of parts of two dresses I sewed together.
